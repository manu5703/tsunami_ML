# Budget / short trips — the most common ride type in NYC, opposite end from training.
# Training zone: fare_amount > 40, trip_distance > 12  (rare premium rides)
# Far zone:      fare_amount < 12, trip_distance < 2   (common short rides, ~35-45% of data)
# Tsunami has coarse/unoptimised partitions here -> higher scan%, lower speedup expected.
