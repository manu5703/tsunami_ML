# GROUP A - Nearby (IN-DISTRIBUTION)
# Tight premium/long-haul zone — strict subset of training zone.
# Training: fare_amount > 30, trip_distance > 8  (~10-15% of data)
# Nearby:   fare_amount > 40, trip_distance > 10 (airport runs, ~5% of data)
# Tsunami has fine-grained partitions in the premium region -> low scan%, high speedup.
